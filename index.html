How to use the site:

Firstly, you'll want to go to "Collection." From there, you can search, by name, the cards you own and add them to your collection. When you've got all the cards ready, you can navigate to the trade page. From there you can put the cards you want to trade and search for the cards your partner wants to trade to you. When you click on the "Match" button, if the price of your cards are greater than your partner's by a certain percentage, we will tell you "Bad Trade." If the price of your cards are lesser than your partner's by a certain percentage, we will tell you "Great Trade!" And if the prices of your cards and your partners cards are close to each other within a certain percentage, then we will tell you "Fair Trade." Additionally, since the Pokemon Trading Card game is famous world-wide, we've added, for your convenience, a currency exchange. Simply pick the currency you would like to have the prices shown as and the site will show you the prices in that currency.
